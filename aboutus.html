About Us

Who We Are?

CarVista, founded in 2020, emerged from humble beginnings with a vision to deliver high-quality vehicles to customers. The organization was established by Lau AHung, who, along with a handful of determined employees, set out to provide exceptional service and expertise in the automotive industry. Presently, CarVista is still led by its original key members.

Over the years, we have honed our expertise in product knowledge, gaining a deep understanding of various vehicle makes and models, including their features, specifications, and performance capabilities.

Additionally, we prioritize excellent customer service to establish strong relationships with our clientele, understand their needs, and offer personalized recommendations.

Mission

At the core of our organization lies a commitment to serving individuals seeking to purchase a car. Our goal is to match each customer with the most suitable vehicle, ensuring their satisfaction with their purchase.



Vision

Moreover, our product offerings extend beyond cars to include a range of car accessories, further enhancing the lives of our customers and communities worldwide. Maintaining unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction remains our long-term objective.



Achievements

CarVista has successfully sold numerous types of cars, including Tesla, Xiaomi, Toyota and Honda. In 2022, our annual sales reached around 800 cars, accompanied by an influx of positive reviews and feedback from satisfied customers. These achievements validate our commitment to delivering exceptional products and services.

Stakeholder

Our organization's stakeholders primarily consist of customers and suppliers. Customers, whether individuals or businesses, play a pivotal role in our operations, as they purchase vehicles and influence industry demand. We prioritize meeting their expectations regarding car quality, pricing, and service. Similarly, suppliers are integral to our business, providing essential parts, accessories, and services to ensure the smooth operation of our dealership.

Future plans

Looking ahead, CarVista aims to expand its product offerings beyond the current selection of Tesla, Xiaomi, and Toyota vehicles. By enhancing our customer service to be more personalized and empathetic, we anticipate attracting a broader customer base seeking the best car-buying experience. Our future endeavors are rooted in our commitment to continuous improvement and providing unparalleled service in the automotive industry.

FAQ❓

What type of car that we sell?

We offer a wide range of vehicles including Xiaomi, Honda, Tesla and Toyota.


Can I schedule a test drive for a car I'm interested in?

Absolutely! You can schedule a test drive by making an appointment through our website. We'll coordinate with you to arrange a convenient time for the test drive.


Do we offer warranties on the car sold through our website?

Warranties are typically offered by the sellers, such as dealerships or manufacturers. Some listings may include warranty information, but it's essential to inquire about warranty options with the seller before purchasing.


How do I know if a car has been involved in any accidents?

Sellers are required to disclose relevant information about a car's history, including accidents, through vehicle history reports such as Carfax or AutoCheck. You can request this information from the seller or conduct your own research using the vehicle identification number (VIN).


What should I do if I have a problem with a car after purchasing it?

If you encounter any issues with a purchased car, we recommend contacting the seller first to discuss possible solutions. If the issue cannot be resolved directly with the seller, we provide assistance and mediation to help reach a satisfactory resolution.
